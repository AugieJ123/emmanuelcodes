Brief write up about Physics Department

Physics department is under the faculty of pure and applied sciences at Fourah Bay College.

The physics department is led by either a Professor or an academic Dr which is regarded as the head of department followed by the departmental admin, senior lecturers and junior staffs.

The physics department building was name after a grate particle physicist by the name of Prof Godwin whose immaculate journey started at the said department. Physics department is one the largest department at the university with two notable buildings, which are, the physics departmental building and the physics lecture theater. The departmental building contains a lecture room, experimental and computer laboratory, lectures offices and more

The department is meant to train brains and nature them into the physics discipline.

The department as of recent expanded beyond its boundaries by doing causes on ICT.

Physics as we all know is a beautiful discipline and even here at Fourah bay college people normally sees physics student as the brains of our generation.

Physics as a discipline has sub specialties and as with regards to this the department at fourah bay college offers modules with accordance to those specialties.

The programs offered at this department are divided into two:

  • Honors
  • General

Students are assigned to these different programs based on their academic performance. The division to these programs starts at the 3rd year of study where students with certain grades as discussed and agreed upon by the departmental administration will be invited to the honors program and those below the said grades will be regarded as general students.
